Port2; controls water reward valve (POW-A) and reward indicator LED (POW-B). In test, POW-A and POW-B get alternatively HIGH and LOW at 0.5 Hz.

Port3; outputs digital signals encoding the current state via DIO-A (data channel) and DIO-B (clock channel). In test, DIO-A and DIO-B get alternatively HIGH and LOW at 1 Hz.

BNC2; receives analog signals encoding treadmill velocity. In test, sample input signal at 100Hz and display  it in a graph pane on the GUI
